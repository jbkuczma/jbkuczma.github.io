Who is James Kuczmarski?

I am a 19 year old computer science major currently attending the State University of New York at Buffalo. The one thing I really enjoy about this field is when I finish writing a program or a piece of code, I can physically show someone and say "I made this." I am a hard working individual who always strives to do my absolute best. In my free time I am an avid photographer, focusing primarily in landscapes and cityscapes. Two of my favorite cities to visit are New York City and Toronto (I hope to travel to San Francisco soon). Currently I work at Delta Sonic Car Wash and have been there for ~1.5 years. Being from Buffalo I am a Bills and Sabres fan even though the past few seasons have been difficult to get through, we will be back in the playoffs very soon! I am also a Knicks fan ... I guess I have a way with picking great teams to root for.
